<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Commenda?">
    Commenda is a business operations management platform that helps companies manage their entities, compliance obligations, indirect tax, documents, and corporate structure. It also connects companies with advisors and service providers for professional services.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who is Commenda for?">
    Commenda is designed for:
    - **Companies** that manage multiple business entities across different jurisdictions
    - **Advisors and accounting firms** that serve corporate clients
    - **Service providers** that deliver incorporation, tax filing, and compliance services
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What countries does Commenda support?">
    Commenda supports entities in 100+ countries. For incorporation services, Commenda currently supports the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Ireland, Singapore, UAE, and India. Compliance tracking and entity management are available for entities in any country.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I manage multiple companies?">
    Yes. If you're involved with multiple companies, you can switch between them using the account switcher. Each company has its own set of entities, team members, and settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get help?">
    You can reach Commenda support by using the in-app chat, emailing support, or contacting your account manager directly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I import my existing entity data?">
    Yes. You can add entities manually through the platform or work with your Commenda account manager to bulk-import entity data from spreadsheets or other systems.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What accounting software does Commenda integrate with?">
    Commenda integrates with QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, Zoho Books, Stripe, Shopify, WooCommerce, and more. See [Supported integrations](/integrations/supported-integrations) for the full list.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the compliance calendar work?">
    When you add an entity and its jurisdiction details, Commenda automatically generates the applicable compliance obligations with their due dates. You can track each filing through its lifecycle and get reminders before deadlines.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
