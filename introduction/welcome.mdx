## Who uses Commenda

Commenda serves three types of users:

- **Companies** — manage your entities, compliance, and taxes through the main platform.
- **Advisors and accountants** — serve your clients, manage service delivery, and collaborate on compliance work.
- **Service providers** — deliver services like incorporation, tax filing, and registered agent work through the agent portal.
