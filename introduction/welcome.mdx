## Who uses Commenda

Commenda serves three types of users:

- **Companies** use the main platform to manage their entities, compliance, and taxes.
- **Advisors and accountants** use the platform to serve their clients, manage service delivery, and collaborate on compliance work.
- **Service providers** use the agent portal to deliver services like incorporation, tax filing, and registered agent work.
