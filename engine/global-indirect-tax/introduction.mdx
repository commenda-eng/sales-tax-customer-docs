## About Commenda

Commenda empowers cross-border companies to streamline and automate their global tax workflows. Our platform simplifies international operations by handling everything from accurate tax rate calculations to automated registration, reporting, and filings.

Commenda's Global Indirect Tax API ensures compliance across multiple jurisdictions, helping businesses avoid costly errors and audits.

For accountants and service providers, we provide the tools and support to expand your practice globally.
