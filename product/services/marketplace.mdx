The **Service marketplace** is where you can browse and purchase professional services for your entities. Services are provided by Commenda's in-house team and vetted external partners.

## Browsing services

Go to **Services** in the sidebar to open the marketplace. Services are organized by country and category.

### Available service categories

- **Incorporation** — form new entities in supported countries
- **Tax filing** — income tax, franchise tax, annual reports
- **Indirect tax** — registration, filing, and compliance
- **Bookkeeping** — ongoing accounting and bookkeeping services
- **Compliance** — regulatory filings, BOI reports, etc.
- **Transfer pricing** — documentation and benchmarking studies
- **Registered agent** — registered agent services by state
- **Legal** — corporate governance, amendments, dissolutions

## Purchasing a service

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a service">
    Browse the marketplace and click on the service you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a variant">
    Some services have different pricing tiers or options. Select the variant that fits your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide details">
    Enter the required information about your entity and the specific work needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and confirm">
    Review the pricing and details, then confirm your order.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once purchased, a service request is automatically created so you can track the progress of the work.

## Service providers

Services may be delivered by:

- **Commenda in-house** — Commenda's own team
- **Commenda advisors** — accounting professionals on the Commenda platform
- **External vendors** — vetted third-party providers

You'll always know who is handling your service and can communicate with them directly through the service request.
