## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Determine nexus">
    Commenda helps you identify where your business has indirect tax nexus based on your registrations, locations, and transaction data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register">
    Register for indirect tax in jurisdictions where you have nexus. Commenda can help you through the registration process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track transactions">
    Connect your accounting software or upload transaction data to track taxable sales.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage exemptions">
    Collect and validate exemption certificates from tax-exempt customers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="File returns">
    Prepare and file your indirect tax returns on time with deadline tracking and filing management.
  </Step>
</Steps>
